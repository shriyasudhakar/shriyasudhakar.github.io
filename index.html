In today’s world of social media, hate is constantly spread

Even in everyday life, such as work and school, hate is spread on a daily basis. Muslims have been targeted and discriminated no matter where they are or what they are doing - this has been going on for several years. Muslim women especially have been facing a lot of discrimination and injustice.

However, we don’t have to keep living in a world like this, and Muslims don’t have to continue living through hateful environments. With change, many Muslims can be given the same opportunities that any other person would be given.

Muslims in general could live happier lives. Even the smallest actions that we take could help make a big difference.

The world could be a better place for everyone in a matter of time. Let’s learn and make an impact!
